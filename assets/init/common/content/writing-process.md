Good sites usually grow from repetition, not from one perfect launch.

Write a little. Publish a little. Adjust the structure when the content asks for it.

Slater is designed to make that loop simple:

- write Markdown
- shape the templates
- rebuild the site
- keep going

This sample post exists to give your homepage a little more shape on first build.
